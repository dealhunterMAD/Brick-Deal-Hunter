# Privacy Policy

**Brick Deal Hunter**

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

## Introduction

Brick Deal Hunter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Push Notification Token**: If you enable notifications, we collect your device's push notification token to send you deal alerts.
- **Notification Preferences**: Your settings for which deals you want to be notified about (discount thresholds, themes, specific sets).

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system version (for app compatibility).
- **App Usage**: Which features you use within the app (for improving the app experience).

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect your name, email address, or personal contact information through the app.
- We do NOT collect payment information or credit card details.
- We do NOT track your location.
- We do NOT collect browsing history outside of the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Send push notifications about LEGO deals matching your preferences
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Maintain and troubleshoot the app

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)
We use Firebase for:
- Cloud database (Firestore) to store deal information
- Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- Analytics to understand app usage

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Expo
We use Expo for app development and push notifications.

Expo's privacy policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

### Affiliate Links
When you tap on a deal to view it at a retailer's website, you will be redirected through affiliate links. These links allow us to earn a small commission if you make a purchase. The retailers may collect their own data according to their privacy policies.

## Data Storage and Security

- Push notification tokens are stored securely in Firebase Firestore
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections

## Your Choices

### Push Notifications
You can disable push notifications at any time through:
- The app's Settings screen
- Your device's notification settings

### Data Deletion
To delete your data, you can:
- Disable notifications in the app (removes your push token from our servers)
- Uninstall the app

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
